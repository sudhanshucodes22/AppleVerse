Explore the New Siri

Intelligence Unveiled

Next-Generation Siri AI

Core Capabilities

Powered by on-device LLMs, Siri processes language naturally, retains context across queries, and understands your files, calendar, and active screen.

psychology Rich Language & Context

Maintains context from one request to the next, understanding references like "it", "them", or "there", and even adapts when you stutter or change your mind mid-sentence.

visibility On-Screen & Personal Context

Siri indexes all data on your device using a semantic memory graph to identify addresses, flight tickets, or files. Screen awareness allows it to fill forms, edit photos, and coordinate in real-time.

keyboard Type to Siri & Voice Sync

Seamlessly transition between voice commands and text entry. Double-tap the bottom of the screen to write queries, ask coding questions, or command smart appliances silently.

Gemini & Apple Intelligence

Two worlds of AI unified. Siri coordinates on-device privacy models with Gemini's massive reasoning power.

Apple Intelligence Engine

Runs a 3-billion parameter on-device model for quick tools, notification triage, and safe semantic lookups.

Google Gemini Integration Cloud Extension

For complex reasoning, document analysis, and broad knowledge queries, Siri integrates directly with Google Gemini. Siri asks for permission before routing requests to ensure privacy control.

How Siri Orchestrates Requests Under the Hood

1. Local Intent Router

Siri first attempts to solve requests locally on-device. Commands like setting alarms, playing music, or writing messages are processed offline instantly.

2. Private Cloud Compute

When advanced processing is needed, Siri leverages private cloud servers running Apple Silicon. Data is cryptographically secure and never saved.

3. Gemini Deep-Dive

For vast search topics, PDF analysis, or coding tasks, Siri dynamically connects to Google Gemini. All transmissions are anonymized and approved by you.
